Welcome to Addison

Addison is a beautiful neighborhood in far nothern Dallas, TX. Addison is known for its numerous restaurants and multiple options for entertaiment. Addison is also home to the Addison Circle Park, a family friendly park that hosts outdoor events from time to time.

Below is a list of some popular spots in Addison:

  • Gloria's Restaurant
  • Sidecar Social
  • Addison Circle Park
  • Spatch
  • Fogo de Chao
